BEST COOKING RECIPE

You will need the following:

  • A meat of your choice
  • Rice
  • American Cheese
  • Soy sauce or beans
  • Eggs

    • STEPS

    Do the following in order:

  • Prepare the eggs however you want
  • Put everything together in a bowl
  • Mix it up with a spoon unti cheese is well-spread
